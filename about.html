Where Work & Waves Converge

The Peninsula was anchored in a simple vision: what if a meal could be a milestone, and a workday could feel like a getaway? We saw the serene dance of the Mtwapa Creek and envisioned a haven where productivity and pleasure could coexist in perfect harmony.

By day, consider us your office with an ocean view. We invite you to dock your laptop on our waterfront deck, where the gentle sea breeze is your new colleague and the panoramic vista your new screensaver. As the sun dips below the horizon, our focus shifts from workflow to flavor flow. The clinking of glasses replaces the clicking of keys, and our kitchen crafts dishes that turn a simple dinner into a memorable occasion.

Peninsula is more than a restaurant; it's a destination built for the ebb and flow of your life. It's your story, set against the backdrop of ours.
