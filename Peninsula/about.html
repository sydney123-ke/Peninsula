About Peninsula

Where Flavor Sets Sail

Nestled on the picturesque Mtwapa Creek, Peninsula isn't just a restaurant; it's an escape. Here, the gentle sea breeze whispers tales of flavor, and every dish is a voyage of discovery. Our wok is our compass, guiding us to create aromatic treasures that capture the soul of coastal cuisine.

A Symphony of the Senses

We orchestrate a daily symphony of the freshest catches. Imagine succulent prawns, tender calamari, and perfectly grilled fish, all singing with a fusion of local spices and international flair. It's more than a meal; it's Mombasa's culinary heritage, reimagined on your plate.

Sip Back and Relax

Our bar is a haven of handcrafted cocktails and refreshing drinks, each one a perfect companion to the stunning views. Whether you're here for a sundowner or a nightcap, our drinks are designed to elevate your experience and toast to the good life.

Your Oasis of Serenity

With panoramic views that stretch to the horizon, Peninsula is a feast for the eyes and the palate. Our serene ambiance, inspired by the natural beauty of the creek, makes every visit a sensory journey. Come, and let us transport you to a world of culinary delight.
